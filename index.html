Event Tracks

Event Tracks

Big Data

With the advent of faster connectivity and greater volume-handling potential around the world, the necessity and knowledge of big data today is an indispensable part of any software developer's repertoire. Along with an application-based workshop, this seminar will cater to students and working professionals alike to boost their real-world experience and thier acclamatization of the subject.

Fintech

The ebbs and highs of the market dynamics might be in chaos right now but your hold on it doesn't have to be. Coupled with the ever increasing demand of working professionals in this interdisciplinary field and the number of Engg+MBA grad professionals in India, this sector is bound to see an upward trend in the near future too. Come join us as we bring to you veterans in the field with invaluable insight into the topic and improve your real-world experience with model development and real-world deployment and unravel how we surf through this maze of technology and finance.

Leadership track

An oft-overlooked topic in technical discussions but probably the most important soft skill of your entire resume characteristic, the necessity  of leadership skills and personality development is perhaps known by all working professional in today's day and age. Learn to lead and manage people with specially challenges and workshops hosted by some of the biggest names in the field and improve your skills manifold.

VR Workshop

The dynamics of our reality might be changing but that doesn't mean your hold on it has to. While often categorized as a niché specalization in developer circles, VR and AR today plays and is bound to play a far more generalized and dominating role following the introduction of the metaverse and blockchain trading. With hands-on software development experience, expert-guided VR hardware building workshops and seminars by prominent individuals in the field, come boost your knowledge and expertise of this subject and get a holistic view on the future possibilities it holds.

Shantiniketan

Destination

Creek Boutique Resort, Shantiniketan

"High achievement always take place in the framework of high expectation."
As the barren branches of trees are slowly giving way to flaming blossoms across the country, the Creek Boutique Resort at Shantiniketan, the official venue of TechX 2022, the biggest techno-management leadership summit of Eastern India, is eagerly awaiting our presence in April with open arms. Nestled in the land of the red soil, with entrancing 'baul' folk songs in the air, Shantiniketan (literally the "Abode of Peace'') is deeply entrenched with the famed legacy and philosophy of Rabindranath Tagore and stands testimony to the rich heritage of Bengal. The picturesque Creek Boutique Resort is the ideal place for those seeking a rich experience and an escape from the polluted city-life into the tranquil lap of Nature. Join us and do give inDigha to the breezy welcome of the auburn Palash trees in full-bloom and experience a unique melange of culture and technology.
